The Insight 03 / 08
The problem is not awareness.
It is activation.

Banking apps are rearview mirrors: they show what happened after the damage is done. Kira changes the model from remember, decide, act to detect, decide, act for me.

01 / CAPTURE

No manual bookkeeping

Voice and receipt inputs become clean transactions and split records in seconds.

02 / SETTLE

No debt chasing

Incoming payments are matched against outstanding shares automatically.

03 / ALLOCATE

No salary drift

Income is split into savings, bills, and flex buckets the moment it arrives.

04 / NUDGE

No vague future

Seasonal savings, squads, and projections make future tradeoffs visible now.

03 / 08
The Solution 04 / 08

Kira is the financial resilience layer inside GXBank.

01

Zero-friction bookkeeping

Voice and receipt photo parsing with structured output for ledger writes.

02

Duit auto-reconciliation

Debt records are fuzzy-matched by sender name and amount when money comes in.

03

Arus salary autopilot

Salary is split into savings, bills, and flex buckets using preset rules.

04

Musim event savings

Raya, PTPTN, semester fees, and festive costs become daily auto-save targets.

05

Kawan + Cermin

Privacy-first squads create accountability. Future-self projections make one habit change feel concrete.

Core Product Thesis
Good money behaviour becomes the default.

Kira does not lecture the user. It removes the tiny decisions where discipline fails: recording, splitting, saving, settling, and planning.
